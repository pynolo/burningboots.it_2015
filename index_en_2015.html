What is Italian Burning Boots

It's a hiking, artistic and naturalistic event... well it will actually be whatever the participants want it to be. Everyone is invited to bring their unique contribution with ideas, creativity and passion.

How it works

From Friday to Sunday

The meeting point will be on Friday on the Apennine Mountains between Tuscany and Emilia. From there we will walk to the hut. On Saturday we'll have the whole day for hiking and on Sunday we'll head back to the meeting point.

Will the hike be physically demanding?

The event is recommended only to those whose phisical conditions are good enough for a mountain hike. The trails will not be hard but they could be challenging for someone.

Participants do everything

There is going to be a limit in the number of participants allowed, so it is important that everyone is willing to do its part. There is no difference between volunteers and participants, we are all both and everyone pays their share :)

What about the night?

Bring some extravagant clothes or just let your best part come out. We trust in irony and self expression!

Principles

Participation

It's not an event for spectators. Get involved, be a volunteer, create and organize. IBB will be different if you are part of it.

Self-reliance

Pack everything you need in your backpack. You are responsible for yourself, mentally and physically. Give yourself what you need - and ask others for help when you need it.

Leave no trace

We don't leave traces of our presence. The area will be left like before the event, or possibly even cleaner. Take all the bags you might need to bring everything back to the car, especially your garbage.

Gifting

We give our time, effort and gifts freely. We give to help others and because it makes us feel good. A workshop, a small badge, a performance... our gifts are from our heart.

No commerce

Forget about money - there's nothing to buy. By removing commerce from our community, we create co-operation and participation.

Self-expression

Be the person you are. Be the person you want to be. We respect and value you, and expect the same from you. You have the freedom to be yourself. Become who you are.

Inclusion

We welcome everyone for their unique contribution to our community. Include others as you want to be included, with respect, consideration and tolerance.

Community & co-operation

A family of individuals, we look after each other. We are united in our need to be part of something larger than ourselves.

Immediacy

Make "now"count. All this will soon be gone, so enjoy now. Experience, participate, be. This is all there is, so seize the moment!

Checklist

We'll be on the top of the mountains, so don't forget WARM clothes.
Some things shouldn't be missing in your backpack! Be careful, items marked with are literally vital!

  • Sleeping bag
  • Good hiking shoes and thick socks*
  • Flashlight
  • Camping mug**
  • Camping dish
  • Camping fork, knife & spoon
  • Water bottle
  • Wet weather gear
  • Sun screen protection & hat
  • Soap & towel
  • Toothbrush & toothpaste
  • Earplugs
  • Sleeping mask
  • No tent is necessary (we have a hut)
* thin socks usually cause blisters
** use a snap-hook to easilly bring it with you

What you CAN'T bring:

  • No pets
  • No sound systems
  • No wrong attitude :)
  • No to everything that's difficult to clean:
  • No glitter
  • No feathers
  • No confetti
  • No explosives or aerial flares

Prohibitions:

  • Cars are not allowed
  • Pets are not allowed
  • It's forbidden to damage and litter the place

FAQ

Will I need to bring my own tent?

No, the amount we ask for joining the event includes the rental of a real mountain hut!
So you will need a sleeping bag but not a tent. Earplugs are recommended too ;)

Don't forget to read the checklist!

Will meals be included?

These are the provided meals:
Friday - dinner.
Saturday - breakfast, lunch (picnic/sandwiches), dinner.
Sunday - breakfast.
We will be the ones to cook for dinner and prepare the picnic, it means you too :)

We will all be the kitchen superstars!

Will there be music?

Maybe, it depends on you too. You'll make everyone happy if you'll bring an instrument or if you want to play! Anyway, we'll be hosted in a natural park therefore any sound system will be forbidden.

Musical instruments and performances are welcome

Will alcoholic drinks be allowed?

It's the same as breakfast: bring your own! For example, if you want some wine or a cuba libre you'll need to bring the ingredients with you! But remember, you'll be responsible for yourself. Don't turn this into a bad experience for you and for anyone else.
